\section{Assessment Scope}

This report evaluates the automated integration and enrichment steps after
cleaning, entity resolution, one-to-one link selection, and unified dataset
construction. The assessment uses hand-labeled candidate pairs as a gold
standard and measures both classifier error and end-to-end survival into the
integrated collection.

\section{Entity Resolution Classifier}

The classifier metrics compare the shipped MongoDB candidate labels against the
out-of-sample hand-labeled gold rows passed with \texttt{--gold-csv}. Manual LLM
labels are treated as overrides when present. Labels passed with
\texttt{--in-calibration-gold-csv} are used for the calibration and
cross-validation block in the generated Markdown report, not for the
out-of-sample score table.

\section{Spatial Enrichment}

Tripadvisor spatial enrichment is measured by comparing geocoded Tripadvisor
coordinates against the Google seed coordinates for human-confirmed matches.
Observed distances are truncated by the entity-resolution blocking radius:
gross geocoding failures usually appear as missed matches, missing coordinate
coverage, or unblockable candidates rather than large distance rows.
